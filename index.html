What to Wear

We can't wait to celebrate with you! To set the mood for the evening, we've chosen a formal dress code. Gentlemen are invited to wear suits; ladies are encouraged to wear floor-length gowns.

In keeping with our vision for the day, we invite you to dress in neutral tones or soft pastels. We kindly ask that bright, vibrant colours be left at home, so that together we may maintain a serene and timeless atmosphere.
